SYMPTOMS OF DIABETES

If you have the following diabetes symtoms , see your doctor about getting your blood sugar tested:

  • Urinate(pee)alot at night
  • Always very thirst
  • lose weight with out individual trying
  • Always hungry
  • Having blurry vision
  • Having numb or tingling hands or feet
  • Feeling very tired
  • Having very dry skin
  • Easly being infected

    • Symptoms of Type 1 Diabetes

    People who have type 1 Diabetes may also have the following symptoms; nausea, Vomiting, or Stomach pains.Type 1 diabetes can be diagnosed at any age, and symptoms can develop in just a few weeks or months and can be severe

    Symptoms of Type 2 Diabetes

    Type 2 diabetes symptoms often take several years to develop. sSome people don't notice any symptoms at all.Type 2 diabetes usually starts when youre an adult, though more and more children and teen are developing it. Because symptoms are hard to notice or spot. its important to know the risk factors for Type 2 diabetes.

    Symptoms of Gestational Diabetes

    Gestational diabetes(diabetes pregnancy) usually doesn't have any symptoms.If your pregnant your doctor should tell you if you for gestational diabetes between 24 and 28 weeks of pregnancy. If needed, you can make changes to protect your health and your baby's health
