TREATMENTS OF DIABETES

Depending on what type of diabetes you have:

  • blood sugar monitoring
  • use of insulin
  • oral drugs may be part of your treatment
  • Eating healthy diet
  • staying at a healthy weight
  • getting regular physical activity
